Add the olive oil to a frying pan (just a little so it coats the bottom of the pan), heat to a medium heat, and add the whisked eggs.

There's some debate on the best way to scramble eggs. Gordon Ramsey apparently suggests a medium heat but take the pan off the heat every 30 seconds.

Personally I (mostly for convenience) use a low-medium heat, but don't take the pan off the heat.

The most important fact though is that scrambling eggs takes time and not to use too high a heat or your eggs with go dry.
